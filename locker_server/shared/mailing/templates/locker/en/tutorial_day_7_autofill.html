Enable autofill to simplify your logins

No more manual typing! See the magic of autofill


Autofill is the key to simplify your login process. Enable Autofill on your mobile app to access all your favorite sites with just one click.



Follow these steps to enable Autofill on your mobile app.

Open the Locker app

Select Menu

Tap Settings

Select Autofill Service

Follow the provided instructions to enable Password Autofill


Here's how to enable Autofill with Locker extension on Chrome:


Open Locker extension on Chrome

Click Settings

Toggle Enable Autofill


That's it! You have successfully enabled Autofill with Locker on your device. Now, Locker can use the credentials saved in the vault to automatically fill in login details and online forms.
