How to get started

1. Create a strong Master Password

Finish by creating a strong Master Password that will protect your vault. Use upper and lower case letters, symbols, and numbers.

What is Master Password?

2. Add your first password (or import passwords)

Click on Add Item to add your first password. Or you can import your previous passwords from browsers or other password managers.

Import & Export data

3. Autofill passwords

Enjoy a secure and seamless browsing experience. Use autofill to login to all your favorite accounts with just a few clicks.

Auto-fill

4. Download Locker app

Download the Locker app from App Store or Google Play Store to bring your passwords anywhere with you.


5. Enable logins with biometrics

Turn on biometric authentication to login quickly and easily.


6. Enable 2FA

Add an additional layer of security by enabling 2FA for your Locker account.
