Did you know you can use biometrics like FaceID/TouchID to login Locker? Your face and fingerprints are the most unique passwords nature ever devised and you can use them to unlock your Locker account. Set it up by going to app’s settings. Available on Android phones and iPhone.

However, biometrics and passwords aren’t considered the same by privacy and data protection legislation. Therefore, use biometrics with caution.
